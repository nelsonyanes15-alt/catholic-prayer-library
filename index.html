Catholic Prayer Library

Lex orandi, lex credendi — the law of prayer is the law of belief. A treasury of the Church's prayers, to learn, to keep, and to pray.

Traditional prayer texts are public domain. Scripture excerpts are from the public-domain World English Bible, Catholic Edition (WEBC). Daily Mass readings, when opened, are displayed live from the USCCB RSS feed. Made for prayer — Ad Majorem Dei Gloriam.
